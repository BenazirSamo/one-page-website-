Our Story

Discover Our Coffee Journey

Founded in 2015, Brew Haven started as a small coffee cart with a big dream - to serve exceptional coffee that brings people together. Our founder, Alex Rivera, a third-generation coffee roaster, wanted to create a space where coffee lovers could enjoy premium brews in a warm, welcoming environment.

Today, we've grown into a beloved neighborhood cafe, but we've never lost sight of our roots. We still roast our beans in small batches and train all our baristas in the art of coffee making.

We're committed to ethical sourcing, working directly with farmers who share our values of sustainability and quality. Every cup tells a story - from the highland farms where our beans are grown to the careful hands that prepare your drink.
